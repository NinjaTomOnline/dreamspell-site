Year-Bearer Colors

What do year-bearer colors mean in Dreamspell?

People usually mean the four Dreamspell color families that shape how a year-bearer reading is talked about. The color language is not separate from the seals. It is part of the same symbolic system people already meet in kin study.

The four color families

RedBegins and initiates movement.
WhiteRefines, bridges, and clarifies.
BlueTransforms and catalyzes change.
YellowMatures, flowers, and ripens expression.

Plain Language

Color is one way people make the yearly signature easier to remember

The color language gives a quick feel for the year-bearer conversation, but it only makes sense when it stays connected to the seal and kin language underneath it.

Red

Initiating

Red language often signals starting, calling in life force, or opening movement.

White

Refining

White language often signals communication, refinement, surrender, or clearing.

Blue

Transforming

Blue language often signals activation, change, catalysis, and movement.

Yellow

Ripening

Yellow language often signals flowering, maturing, illuminating, or completing expression.
