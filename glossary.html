Quick Reference

What the main Dreamspell terms mean

If you only want the shortest version: tones describe movement, seals describe quality, kin combines them, and the moons give the annual rhythm.

Kin

A kin is one daily signature formed by a galactic tone and a solar seal.

Tzolkin

The Tzolkin is the 260-day cycle created by combining 13 tones with 20 seals.

Wavespell

A wavespell is the 13-day arc created by moving through all thirteen tones in sequence.

13 Moon year

The Dreamspell year is commonly studied as thirteen 28-day moons with their own named rhythm.
