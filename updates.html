Release Timeline

A short timeline of the current release track

This timeline is intentionally short and public-facing. It highlights the product and site improvements that matter to future users.

April 14, 2026

Dreamspell is live on the App Store

The public site now points directly at the live Dreamspell App Store listing for iPhone and iPad, and the Get App page has been converted from launch-prep language into a real download hub.

April 13, 2026

Learning hub expansion

The public site now includes a stronger beginner path, a Why Dreamspell explainer, a searchable glossary, and clearer routes between the guide, FAQ, glossary, and app page.

April 12, 2026

Live domain and launch-path cleanup

The public site moved onto its live custom domain, the launch-update path became clearer, and the mobile navigation was tightened for real phone use.

April 11, 2026

Core relaunch scope locked in

Dreamspell settled around Today, Calendar, Kin Explorer, Journal, and Resources, with offline-first study tools, local journaling, iPhone and iPad layouts, and English and Spanish support.

What is part of the current promise

  • daily kin lookup and calendar study
  • Kin Explorer for tones, seals, and combinations
  • local journaling tied to dates and kin
  • bundled reference content and learning material
  • iPhone and iPad support
  • English and Spanish localization

What is not being promised at launch

  • no active contacts import in the current version
  • no account system for the current feature set
  • no server-backed journal sync in the current version
